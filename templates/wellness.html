What is Mindful Interval?

Taking structured breaks using intervals (active/rest) prevents cognitive burnout. Set an active duration (e.g., 5 min meditation or stretching) and a rest duration before the next round begins. This is critical for neuroplasticity and maintaining deep focus throughout long study days.
